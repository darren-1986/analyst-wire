How to Use the Radar

Understanding the Columns

Bias: Indicates market direction (Bullish/Bearish/Neutral). Prioritize trades in the direction of the bias.

Bull/Bear Line: Your key pivot point. Above = Bullish sentiment, Below = Bearish sentiment.

Market State: "Trending" (momentum based) or "Consolidated" (range-bound). Adjust strategy accordingly.

Volatility: The range distance (R1 - S1). Higher numbers indicate a wider trading range.

Trader's Workflow

  1. Filter by Category/Ticker to find your asset.
  2. Identify the Bias to know which direction to trade.
  3. Use the Bull/Bear Line to validate your entries.
  4. Respect the Volatility metrics for stop-loss sizing.
