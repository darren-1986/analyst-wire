How to Use the Market Trade Radar

A professional-grade terminal guide to decoding intraday market structures and identifying top-tier trade ideas.

1. Structural Component Matrix

BIAS (5-Tier Framework): Highlights current market direction and institutional momentum relative to the central pivot area.

  • ▲ Strong Bullish: Heavy buying pressure. Look for momentum continuation.
  • ▲ Medium Bullish: Moderate upward bias. Approaching pivot boundaries.
  • ➔ Neutral: Price is resting directly on or near the central pivot corridor. Equilibrium phase.
  • ▼ Medium Bearish: Moderate downward bias. Testing pivot floors.
  • ▼ Strong Bearish: Heavy distribution/selling pressure. Look for short continuations.

Bull/Bear Line: The primary institutional pivot point for the day. If the market is trading above this number, sentiment is fundamentally long; below this number, sentiment is fundamentally short.

Market State & Volatility Engine: Automatically measures the absolute distance between your primary intraday support (S1) and resistance (R1).

  • Trending: Volatility has expanded past standard asset true ranges (e.g., >75 pips on major FX, >80 points on Indices, >$15 on Gold). Expect continuous follow-through.
  • Consolidated: Volatility is compressed inside standard ranges. Expect range-bound, choppy, or mean-reverting price action.

2. Playbook: How to Find Trade Ideas

💡 Strategy A: The Breakout / Momentum Play

  • The Setup: Look for assets displaying a Strong Bullish or Strong Bearish Bias combined with a TRENDING Market State.
  • Execution: Avoid fading these moves. Instead, filter for these tickers, open your charts, and look for pullback entries (e.g., buying a minor dip toward the Bull/Bear Line in a Strong Bullish trend) to ride the expansion wave.

💡 Strategy B: The Range Reversion Play

  • The Setup: Look for assets displaying a Neutral, Medium Bullish, or Medium Bearish Bias combined with a CONSOLIDATED Market State.
  • Execution: These markets are trapped in ranges. Look for short opportunities at R1 / R2 or long opportunities at S1 / S2, targeting a price return back to the central Bull/Bear Line.

💡 Strategy C: The Dynamic Pivot Shift

  • The Setup: Monitor assets carrying a Neutral bias. This means the market is sitting dead on its institutional pivot line.
  • Execution: This is a coiled spring. Watch for a directional breakout away from the Bull/Bear Line on your short-term execution charts to capture the early inception of a fresh trend.

How to Use the Order Flow Strategy Engine

A tactical guide to trading large institutional option expiry levels.

Understanding Dynamic Expiry Levels

This module tracks massive institutional option expiries. These strikes act as magnetic "gravity wells" for price action. The engine dynamically calculates the volume against the specific asset class (e.g., a 600m order is massive for AUD/USD, but standard for EUR/USD) to dictate how the market will behave at that level.

  • INSTITUTIONAL WALL:
    Extreme, top-tier institutional defense is anticipated for this specific currency pair. Institutions are heavily incentivized to defend this strike. Expect a sharp rejection.
    Strategy: Fade the approach. The engine calculates a defensive 20-pip Stop Loss behind the wall, targeting a 50-pip mean reversion.
  • MAJOR CLUSTER:
    Heavy market liquidity. The level will cause a significant reaction and chop, but may not hold as a permanent wall.
    Strategy: Scalp the rotation. The engine calculates a 15-pip Stop Loss, targeting a 30-pip bounce.
  • MINOR CLUSTER:
    Standard liquidity. Expect minor stalling before trend continuation.
    Strategy: The engine calculates a tight 10-pip stop, targeting 20 pips.

Automated Buy vs Sell Execution

Because the engine is fed live Spot Prices alongside the Strike Expiries, it knows exactly which direction the market is approaching the level from.

  • If Strike > Spot Price: Price is rallying up. The level is designated as RESISTANCE (SELL).
  • If Strike < Spot Price: Price is dropping down. The level is designated as SUPPORT (BUY).

The system will instantly output the exact Stop Loss and Take Profit prices so you can place limits directly into your brokerage without manual math.
